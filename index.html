The Chair Project

Building a true circular economy for Vienna

More About the Vision

The City Project is inviting Vienna-based creatives and small brands to reimagine the city's waste. We work with artists, students, and small brands to turn discarded materials into thoughtful, useful things — right here in Vienna.

Our model is simple: local materials, local collaborations, limited output — and real creativity in how we reuse.

The Problem & Our Solution

With the rising waste in Vienna, we saw an opportunity: to turn the city’s waste into a creative playground for sustainable design. By collecting smart, repulsing the materials and repurposing them into new products, we make sustainability tangible and exciting.

What Drives Us

Our drive is to use the local resources (“trash”) while strengthening the local brands.

Who We Are

Repurposing is a field to develop — and this is exactly what we want to perfect. We specialize in materials and are committed to exploring how reuse can grow and be implemented back into the local economy.

For us, the closest thing to true sustainability is local, smart, small-scale solutions. And we want to make that approach easy and “normal” for everyone to adopt.

Our Process

We collect discarded materials from across Vienna — fabric, wood, metal, objects.

You can add an application of your product or idea, and we will help you think how to implement repurposed materials into it.

Right now, we’re going out with a small campaign featuring bags made of repurposed leather from different discarded objects.
