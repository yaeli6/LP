Design something that matters — with what Vienna left behind.

The City Project is inviting Vienna-based creatives and small brands to reimagine the city's waste. We’ll provide the materials, the vision, and the platform — you bring the ideas.

What We're About

We’re launching a series of limited design collaborations using 100% reclaimed materials found in Vienna — from old furniture and fabrics to street-found oddities.

We’re looking for people who want to build something with real meaning, style, and local impact.

What You Get:

  • Access to curated, clean recycled materials
  • A platform to launch your designs under a shared label
  • Visibility through pop-ups, exhibitions, and editorial content
  • A community of like-minded creatives

Who We're Looking For:

  • Independent designers (fashion, furniture, object)
  • Small sustainable brands
  • Artists and art students
  • Craft-makers (leatherworkers, tailors, upcyclers)
  • Photographers, stylists, and storytellers
