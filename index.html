The Chair Project

Building a true circular economy for Vienna

Our Vision

The City Project is inviting Vienna-based creatives and small brands to reimagine the city's waste. We work with artists, students, and local makers to turn discarded materials into thoughtful, useful things — right here in Vienna.

Our model is simple: local materials, local collaborations, limited output — and real creativity in how we reuse.

Why We Started

With the rising waste in Vienna, we saw an opportunity: to turn the city’s overlooked materials into a creative playground for sustainable design. By collecting smart, repurposing with care, and rethinking design from the ground up — we aim to build a model of what a circular city can look like.

What Drives Us

Our goal is to use the local resources (“trash”) while strengthening the small brands that shape the creative life of this city.

Who We Are

Repurposing is a craft — and we’re here to help develop it. We specialize in materials and collaborate with others to explore new ways of reuse.

This project is a field lab: for exploring, experimenting, and making reuse feel both normal and inspiring. It's not about selling sustainability. It's about making it work — locally, beautifully, practically.

Our Process

We collect discarded materials from across Vienna — fabric, wood, metal, objects.

Collaborators can submit a product or idea, and we’ll work with you to think about how to bring reused materials into it.

Right now, we’re launching a small line of bags made from repurposed leather — cut from objects that were headed for the landfill.
